My big trip to Montenegro Scenes from Montenegro
Montenegro is a country located in the west-central Balkans
at the southern end of the Dinaric Alps.
It is bounded by the Adriatic Sea and Croatia to the southwest,
Bosnia and Herzegovina to the northwest, Serbia to the northeast,
Kosovo to the east, and Albania to the southeast.

The Bay of Kotor is the largest Adriatic bay between Montenegro and Croatia.

Gabela street is located in the town of Risan on the Bay of Kotor.
It dates from the 12th century and is constructed from river rocks.

The walls and structures in the Bay of Kotor are built from rocks
mined in local quaries.
Many walls are centuries old and still stand today
